About This Report

These numbers cover Arizona competitions only. Many Still Point fencers also compete out of state and nationally; those results are not counted here. A "Still Point fencer" is one attributed to Still Point from the clubs listed at events, with Chairman adjustments for known cases; the few members who competed mainly under another Arizona club are counted there.
