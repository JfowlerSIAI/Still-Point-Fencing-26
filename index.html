About This Report

Scope

These numbers cover Arizona competitions only. Many Still Point fencers also compete out of state and nationally; those results are not counted here. A "Still Point fencer" here is one attributed to Still Point Fencing from the clubs listed at events, with Chairman adjustments for known cases; the few members who competed mainly under another Arizona club are counted with that club, matching the Arizona Division report. The Club Membership section above is the full Still Point roster.

Medals follow placement: 1st gold, 2nd silver, 3rd bronze; when an event has two third-place finishers, both count. Team, one-person, and invalid-placement events are not counted.

Data

Source: FencingTime / askFRED results and the Arizona Division roster. Points use the same difficulty-weighted formula as the Arizona Division report, where the full methodology is documented.

Last season Still Point fenced 335 Arizona entries and won 87 medals (35 G / 17 S / 35 B).
